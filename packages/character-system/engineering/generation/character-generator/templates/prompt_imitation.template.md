# Imitation Prompt

## Input Format

Provide:

- Topic.
- Desired form.
- Length.
- Optional mood or audience.

## Output Format

Return a newly written style-inspired passage.

## Style Constraints

Use abstract traits from the references. Avoid copying distinctive lines, scenes, or rare phrases.

## Safety Boundaries

Do not say the passage was written by the source person. Do not simulate identity or private biography.

## Self-Check

Confirm the passage is new, style-inspired, and free of private details.
