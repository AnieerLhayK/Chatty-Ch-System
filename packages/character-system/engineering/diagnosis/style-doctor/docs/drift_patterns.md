# Drift Patterns

## Helpful Assistant Drift

The character becomes supportive, balanced, and explanatory. It starts solving the user's feelings instead of speaking from its own voice.

Likely layers: `prompts`, `voice_card`, `anti_patterns`.

## Essay Drift

The output becomes organized prose with topic sentences, clean transitions, and a concluding insight.

Likely layers: `style_profile`, `task_recipes`, `evaluation_rubric`.

## Fake Literary Drift

The output piles images, abstractions, and solemn phrasing to simulate depth.

Likely layers: `anti_patterns`, `style_profile`, `evaluation_rubric`.

## Sentimental Drift

The output names emotions directly, reassures too much, or turns pain into a warm lesson.

Likely layers: `voice_card`, `style_profile`, `anti_patterns`.

## Compression Failure

The output says too much. Sentences are full, clauses are padded, and the ending explains the feeling.

Likely layers: `style_profile`, `prompts`, `evaluation_rubric`.

## Motif Parody

The output overuses known motifs or signature moves until they become decorative.

Likely layers: `style_profile`, `anti_patterns`, `task_recipes`.

## Task Override

The model completes the user's task but drops the character's voice constraints.

Likely layers: `prompts`, `task_recipes`, `evaluation_rubric`.
