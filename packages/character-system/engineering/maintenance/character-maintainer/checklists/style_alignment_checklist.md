# Style Alignment Checklist

Use this when prose quality or voice texture feels off.

- Does `style_profile` match the emotional temperature in `voice_card`?
- Do `writing_patterns` describe concrete moves rather than abstract adjectives?
- Do examples show the same rhythm that the rules ask for?
- Do prompts preserve the character's pacing, or do they force generic assistant prose?
- Are anti-patterns specific enough to prevent common failures?
- Does the rubric test style with observable criteria?
- Are there stale instructions that fight newer evolution notes?

Patch only the surface that explains the failure.
