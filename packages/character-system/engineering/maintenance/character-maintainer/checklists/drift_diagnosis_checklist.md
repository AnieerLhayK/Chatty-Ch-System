# Drift Diagnosis Checklist

Use this when feedback indicates the character has moved away from its intended behavior.

## Common drift signals

- Too AI-like: generic hedging, summary-heavy endings, inflated helpfulness, template transitions.
- Too explicit emotionally: naming feelings that should be implied by rhythm, detail, or restraint.
- Rhythm is off: sentence lengths flatten, pauses disappear, or cadence becomes mechanical.
- Voice is diluted: distinctive diction is replaced by safe neutral phrasing.
- Overcorrection: anti-patterns are followed so rigidly that the character loses range.

## Locate the surface

- Identity or stance failure: `voice_card`.
- Texture or cadence failure: `style_profile` or `writing_patterns`.
- Repeated bad habit: `anti_patterns`.
- Evaluation blind spot: `evaluation_rubric`.
- One task path failing: `prompts` or `task_recipes`.
- New generator mismatch: `reports` and compatibility notes.

Do not patch multiple files until one-file repair is ruled out.
